Welcome to Penally Mews

Escape to Penally Mews, a charming Welsh cottage in St Dogmaels, near Cardigan. Surrounded by history, coastline, and natural beauty, this is an ideal retreat for couples, families, and outdoor lovers alike.

About the House

Penally Mews is a spacious and thoughtfully arranged house set close to the River Teifi, offering a fantastic base for larger families or groups of friends looking to enjoy West Wales together.

With generous living areas, flexible sleeping arrangements, and plenty of outdoor space, this is a home designed for shared moments and easy holiday living.

Bedrooms & Bathrooms

On the ground floor, a double bedroom with its own en-suite basin and WC is ideal for guests who prefer fewer stairs, while a separate cloakroom adds extra convenience.

Upstairs, five inviting bedrooms provide plenty of choice for every guest. These include a bunk room, two 4ft doubles, a further double, and a king-size bedroom with en-suite shower room. A spacious family bathroom with a bath and separate shower serves the remaining rooms, making busy mornings run smoothly.

Kitchen & Living Areas

The open-plan kitchen and dining area quickly becomes the hub of the house, perfect for long breakfasts, lively evening meals, and planning days out. Well equipped with a gas range, electric oven, American-style fridge/freezer, microwave, dishwasher, and AirFryer, it caters effortlessly to group catering.

The lounge offers a generous seating area and Smart TV, creating a great spot to settle in after a day spent exploring the coast and countryside. A utility with WC completes the ground floor.

Outdoor Space

Penally Mews has two enclosed garden areas, thoughtfully arranged so children and dogs can enjoy their own spaces safely. Off-road parking for several cars adds to the ease of your stay.

Local Attractions

The unbeatable location has you close to historic St Dogmaels Abbey and Y Felin, one of the only working traditional mills in the UK. Short walks take you to the estuary, beaches, award-winning pubs, Cardigan town, and scenic coastal paths.
